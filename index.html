About KMJ Catering & Events

Founded by Mojisola, KMJ Catering & Events is a premier catering and event planning service based in Nigeria. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we've perfected the art of creating memorable events through delicious food and impeccable service.

Our passion for culinary excellence and attention to detail sets us apart. We believe every event is unique and deserves a personalized touch, whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand celebration.
